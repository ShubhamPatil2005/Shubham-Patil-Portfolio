Landing Page for HR Help Desk

Project information :

Overview :

  • The vision before designing this landing page was to simplify the whole HR Management function by making it highly usable to everyone not just for the HR itself.
  • The main purpose was also to provide a better user experience to the employee.

    • Case Study :

  • In any organisation the most important task is to provide and manage all the Human Resource. As we know, it is a difficult task to manage large amount man power and fulfill their necessities. The design of this lnding page is a trial of improving the user experience and to minimizing the difficulties of the task.
  • The challenge is about taking things that are infinitely complex and making them simpler and more understandable.
  • In this design I have tried to design a Dashboard specially for the HR Departmaent, from where they can handle all the operations like hiring, interview schedules, salary distribution, event management etc.
  • This design can also be benificial for the employees to create their profile and to connect quickly to the HR Departmaent.
